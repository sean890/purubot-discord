**`paimon! weapon/p! w`**
Search for weapon information with a keyword!

***Parameters***
`[keyword]`: Search for keywords of the weapon you wish to display, can be its name, rarity, weapon type, secondary stat, effect, location

***Usages***
`p! w [keyword]`

***Examples***
Search by:
Name - `p! w prototype animus`
Rarity - `p! w 5*`
Weapon type - `p! w sword`
Secondary stat - `p! w energy recharge`
Effect - `p! w increases atk`
Location - `p! w craftable`
