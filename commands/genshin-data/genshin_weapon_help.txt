**`~g weapon/~g w`**
Search for weapon information with a keyword!

***Parameters***
`[keyword]`: Search for keywords of the weapon you wish to display, can be its name, rarity, weapon type, secondary stat, effect, location

***Usages***
`~g w [keyword]`

***Examples***
Search by:
Name - `~g w prototype animus`
Rarity - `~g w 5*`
Weapon type - `~g w sword`
Secondary stat - `~g w energy recharge`
Effect - `~g w increases atk`
Location - `~g w craftable`
